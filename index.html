Coronavirus Dashboard

About

This project was developed to display the Coronavirus statistics for Ireland in an easy to use dashboard. Statistics can be analysed through graphs and an interactive map of Ireland. Feel free to sign up to the daily newsletter to receive updates by email.

Graphs

To get up to date info on the Coronavirus figures for Ireland with details on the latest confirmed cases, deaths and recoveried for each day and since the virus arrived in Ireland. There is also confirmed cases breakdown by age, gender, transmission and hospitalisation.

Interactive Map

Use the button below to see the breakbown for each county of Ireland. Hover over a county to see the confirmed cases, deaths and recoveries for that county.
